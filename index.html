About Me

HIT (Weihai) · Automation

An Automation Student Passionate About Technology

I am Xiuyi Yang, an undergraduate student in Automation at Harbin Institute of Technology (Weihai). I have a strong passion for embedded systems, computer vision, and intelligent control.

During my time at university, I have participated in multiple national competitions including the National College Electronic Design Contest and the Marine Vehicle Design and Making Competition, gaining extensive hands-on project experience. I am proficient in C programming, and skilled with Raspberry Pi, OpenCV, PID control, Bluetooth communication, and HSV visual recognition.

I served as a member of the Student Union at the School of Information Science and Engineering, where I was recognized as an "Outstanding Member." I have also received multiple university-level scholarships.
